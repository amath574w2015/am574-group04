Instructions:
Navigate to the sqrtflux directory, and then input into command line:
$ make .exe
$ make .output
$ make .plots

Comments:
The square root flux is implemented in the Riemann solver 'rp1_sqrtflux.f90' through the Rankine-Hugoniot shock speed.

As the wave propagates to the right, a shock develops on the backside of the pulse, and a rarefaction wave develops on the leading edge of the pulse.
